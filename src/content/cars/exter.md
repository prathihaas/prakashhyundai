## Overview
Hyundai EXTER Knight symbolizes outdoor, travel and leisure, drawing inspiration from the natural world with a modern take on SUV design. It features bold styling with red highlights, advanced technology like a voice-enabled sunroof and dual-camera dashcam, spacious interiors with more legroom, and efficient powertrains including petrol and bi-fuel CNG options, ensuring a blend of style, innovation, and performance for urban and outdoor lifestyles.
## Key Specifications
- **Engine:** 1.2 L Kappa Petrol or 1.2 L Bi-Fuel Kappa Petrol with CNG (4-cylinder)
- **Transmission:** 5-speed Manual (for both petrol and CNG) or Smart Auto AMT (for petrol)
- **Fuel Type:** Petrol, Bi-Fuel Petrol-CNG
- **Safety Features:** Over 40 advanced features including ESC, VSM, HAC, TPMS, and dual-camera dashcam
