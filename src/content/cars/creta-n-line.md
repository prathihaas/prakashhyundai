## Overview
The Hyundai CRETA N Line is a performance-oriented SUV powered by a 1.5l turbo petrol engine, featuring sporty accents, bold silhouette inspired by N Line styling, and advanced technology for an exhilarating drive. It offers a thrilling experience with finely tuned suspension, road-gripping alloys, and interiors fused with performance and precision, including sporty black themes with red inserts and N logo accents.
## Key Specifications
- **Engine:** 1.5l turbocharged petrol engine
- **Transmission:** 7-Speed DCT / 6-Speed Manual
- **Power:** 160 PS
- **Fuel Type:** Petrol
