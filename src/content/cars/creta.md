## Overview
The Hyundai CRETA King Limited Edition, based on the King Knight trim, exudes sophistication and authority. Available in Abyss Black, Atlas White, and Black Matte colours, it comes powered by a 1.5L MPi Petrol (iVT) and 1.5L U2 CRDi Diesel (6-speed AT) engine. Combining exclusive design elements with advanced comfort and technology, this edition redefines premium SUV appeal.
## Key Specifications
- **Engine:** 1.5L MPi Petrol (iVT) or 1.5L U2 CRDi Diesel (6-speed AT)
- **Transmission:** IVT or 6-speed AT
- **Fuel Type:** Petrol or Diesel
