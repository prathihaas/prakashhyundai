## Overview
The Hyundai ALCAZAR is a versatile 6 and 7 seater SUV designed for dynamic lifestyles, seamlessly blending power, comfort, and style. It offers spacious premium interiors, energetic performance for various terrains, and thoughtful features like flexible seating, storage solutions, and advanced technology to elevate journeys from city streets to adventures.
## Key Specifications
- **Engine:** Choice of 1.5-litre turbo petrol or 1.5-litre diesel
- **Transmission:** Choice of MT, AT, and DCT
- **Power:** 160 PS
- **Fuel Type:** Petrol or diesel
