Toyota Glanza attracts buyers because of the Toyota badge — Japan reliability, strong service network, trusted brand. But there is a fundamental truth about the Glanza that changes the comparison entirely: **The Toyota Glanza is manufactured by Maruti Suzuki.** It is a Baleno with a Toyota badge.

Understanding this changes the comparison from "Toyota vs Hyundai" to "rebadged Maruti vs purpose-built Hyundai."

---

## What Is the Toyota Glanza Really?

The Toyota Glanza is part of a technology-sharing partnership between Toyota and Maruti Suzuki:
- **Built at:** Maruti Suzuki's Manesar plant (same as Baleno)
- **Platform:** Maruti Heartect (same as Baleno)
- **Engine:** Maruti K12N 1.2L (same engine as Baleno)
- **Gearbox:** Maruti 5-speed MT / AMT (same as Baleno)
- **Manufacturing quality:** Maruti quality standards
- **Toyota adds:** Badge, slightly different grille, Toyota dealership network

The Glanza's performance, mileage, interior, safety structure, and driving characteristics are identical to the Baleno. Because they are, literally, the same car.

You do NOT get:
- Better engine
