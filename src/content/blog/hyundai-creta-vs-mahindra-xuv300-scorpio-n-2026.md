## Where Scorpio N Wins

Scorpio N has genuine advantages for specific use cases:
- Off-road capability: 4WD option, rugged suspension
- Presence and intimidation factor on highways
- Mahindra's farm/agricultural connection — emotional resonance in rural Telangana

For buyers who genuinely need off-road capability, Scorpio N makes sense. For everyday family use, Creta is the better car.
