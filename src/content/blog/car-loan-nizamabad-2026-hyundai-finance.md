## Understanding Car Loan Basics

### On-Road Price vs Ex-Showroom Price

The loan is calculated on the **on-road price** (not just ex-showroom). In Telangana, on-road price is typically 12–15% higher than ex-showroom due to:

- **Road tax:** 9–11% of ex-showroom price (Telangana)
- **Registration charges:** ₹5,000–12,000 depending on vehicle category
- **Hyundai insurance (comprehensive):** ₹15,000–50,000 depending on car value
- **Accessories / extended warranty:** Optional

**Example:** Hyundai Creta SX petrol (₹15.99L ex-showroom)
- Road tax ~₹1.75L
- Registration ~₹8,000
- Insurance ~₹35,000
- **On-road price: ~₹18.1–18.4L**

## How to Improve Your Car Loan Terms

### 1. Improve Your CIBIL Score
A credit score above 750 can reduce your interest rate by 0.5–1.5%, saving ₹30,000–60,000 on a ₹15L loan over 5 years. Check your CIBIL score free at cibil.com before applying.

### 2. Larger Down Payment
A larger down payment (30–40% instead of 20%) reduces:
- Loan amount and total interest paid
- Monthly EMI burden
- Risk for the bank (may get better rates)

### 3. Shorter Tenure
Longer tenure = lower EMI but more total interest. Compare:
- 60-month loan on ₹12L at 9%: Total interest = ₹2.9L
- 84-month loan on ₹12L at 9%: Total interest = ₹4.1L
**Saving by choosing 60 months: ₹1.2 lakh**

### 4. Apply Through Authorized Dealer
Applying for HMF through Prakash Hyundai (not independently) often gets better scheme rates, especially during festival and year-end periods.
