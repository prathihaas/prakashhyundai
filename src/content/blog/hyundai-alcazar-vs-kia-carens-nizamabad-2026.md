## Why Hyundai Alcazar Wins for Nizamabad Families

This is the most important point in this comparison. The Kia Carens, despite being a premium family MPV/SUV at ₹10–20 lakh, offers **zero ADAS features across any variant in 2026**. No forward collision warning. No lane keep assist. No automatic emergency braking.

The Hyundai Alcazar Prestige(O) and Signature variants come with Level 2 ADAS:
- Forward collision avoidance (FCA)
- Lane keeping assist (LKA)
- Blind spot collision warning (BCW)
- Rear cross-traffic collision warning (RCCW)
- Driver attention warning (DAW)

For a family of 7 driving from Nizamabad to Hyderabad (180km), ADAS safety features are not optional. They are the difference between a family car and a safe family car.
