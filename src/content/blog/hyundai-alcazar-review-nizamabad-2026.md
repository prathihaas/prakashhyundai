# Hyundai Alcazar Review — 7-Seater Family SUV for Telangana 2026

Large joint families in Telangana have a specific car problem — most SUVs seat 5, but a typical Nizamabad family outing with grandparents, parents, and children needs 6–7 seats. The [Hyundai Alcazar](/en/cars/alcazar) is built precisely for this scenario.

Built on the same platform as the Creta but stretched and raised, the Alcazar adds a full third row and more space while keeping the Hyundai quality and technology that buyers trust.

## Why the Alcazar Makes Sense in Nizamabad

Nizamabad has a strong joint family culture. Family events — weddings, pilgrimages to Nizamabad's temples, trips to Hyderabad for medical appointments — often involve 6–7 people travelling together. Before the Alcazar, the only options were:

- A premium MPV like the Innova Crysta (₹20L+, boxy design)
- Two separate cars (expensive and inconvenient)
- Stuffing 7 into a 5-seater (unsafe and uncomfortable)

The Alcazar solves this cleanly at ₹16.78L–₹21L.

---

## Three Rows — Comfort Assessment

### First Row
Wide, comfortable front seats with good bolstering. Driver gets height-adjustable seat and lumbar support. Power adjustable on top variants.

### Second Row
The Alcazar offers a choice: **6-seater** (captain seats in row 2) or **7-seater** (bench seat in row 2).

Captain seats (6-seater) give each second-row passenger their own armrest and are more comfortable. The bench (7-seater) fits an extra person.

For Nizamabad families, we recommend the **6-seater captain configuration** — the extra comfort for second-row passengers (often elderly family members) is worth the one-seat sacrifice.

### Third Row
Honest assessment: the third row is suitable for children or short adults on trips up to 1 hour. For a 2.5-hour Nizamabad–Hyderabad drive, adults in the third row will feel cramped. This is normal for this segment — even the Toyota Fortuner has similar third-row limitations.

**Practical tip:** Use the third row for children, and let the adults sit in rows 1 and 2.

## 200mm Ground Clearance — Best in This Review

The Alcazar's 200mm ground clearance is the highest among Hyundai cars in Nizamabad. This makes it particularly well-suited for:
- Rural roads near Bodhan, Armoor, Balkonda mandals
- Monsoon season when roads flood temporarily
- Village wedding venues with unpaved access

## Engine Options

### 1.5L Turbo Petrol (160 PS)
The livelier option. 7-speed DCT makes city driving smooth. Despite the extra weight (the Alcazar is heavier than the Creta), the turbo petrol feels adequately powerful. Best for buyers who prioritize driving engagement.

**Real-world mileage:** 12–14 km/l city, 15–17 km/l highway

### 1.5L Diesel (116 PS)
For large families who cover significant distances — 2,000+ km/month — the diesel is the economical choice. 20.4 km/l ARAI means significantly lower monthly fuel bills.

The diesel is particularly popular among Nizamabad-based business owners who use the Alcazar as a family-and-business vehicle.

**Real-world mileage:** 17–19 km/l city, 20–22 km/l highway
