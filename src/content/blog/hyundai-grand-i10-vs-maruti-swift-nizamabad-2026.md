## Price — Grand i10 Nios Starts Cheaper

The Grand i10 Nios starts ₹57,000 less than the Swift. This matters for budget buyers. At entry level, the Nios is the more accessible choice.

At the top end, the Swift goes to ₹9.75L while the Nios tops at ₹8.98L — again, the Nios is cheaper.

**Advantage: Grand i10 Nios** (lower price across the range)

---

## Mileage — Swift's Major Advantage

The Maruti Swift's new Z-series engine achieves a remarkable 23.96 km/l ARAI. The Grand i10 Nios achieves 20.7 km/l — still excellent, but notably less efficient.

Real-world difference in Nizamabad:
- Swift: 18–20 km/l city
- Grand i10 Nios: 15–17 km/l city

This is a meaningful difference over years of ownership. At 1,200 km/month:
- Swift costs: ~₹6,600/month (at ₹104/litre)
- Nios costs: ~₹7,500/month
- **Monthly saving with Swift: ~₹900** → ~₹54,000 over 5 years

If fuel economy is your top priority, the Swift wins convincingly.

**Advantage: Swift** (significantly better fuel economy — real money over time)

---

## Ride Comfort — Advantage to Grand i10 Nios

This is Hyundai's traditional strength. The Grand i10 Nios rides more comfortably over Nizamabad's mix of smooth roads and rough patches. The suspension absorbs bumps with more compliance.

The Swift's suspension is firmer — tuned for a more sporty feel. Younger drivers love this; families and elders may find it less comfortable over long distances.

For Nizamabad to Hyderabad drives on NH44, the Nios is noticeably more relaxed in the cabin.

**Advantage: Grand i10 Nios** (more comfortable for families and longer trips)

---

## Driving Engagement — Swift Wins

If you love driving, the Swift is more fun. The firmer suspension, precise steering, and that sporty feel make it rewarding to drive spiritedly. It is no coincidence the Swift is also available as the Swift Sport in some markets.

The Nios is adequate but not exciting to drive.

**Advantage: Swift** (more fun to drive, better for driving enthusiasts)

---

## Interior and Features

Both are basic at entry level, but both offer:
- 7-inch touchscreen (Swift) / 8-inch touchscreen (Nios)
- Android Auto and Apple CarPlay
- Manual AC
- Height-adjustable seat (driver)

The Grand i10 Nios has a slightly larger touchscreen (8-inch vs 7-inch) and the cabin design feels more modern in mid-to-top variants.

**Advantage: Marginal Nios** (larger screen, slightly more modern design)

---

## CNG Variants — Both Available

Both offer CNG variants with exceptional running economy. The Nios CNG achieves 31.59 km/kg vs Swift CNG's 30.90 km/kg — very similar. For daily commuters in Nizamabad who can access CNG, either variant makes financial sense.

**Advantage: Tie** (both excellent CNG options)
