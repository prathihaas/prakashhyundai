## Bodhan Driving Environment

Bodhan's roads include:
- **Bodhan–Nizamabad NH**: Excellent highway — 30km drive, all Hyundais comfortable
- **Bodhan town roads**: Mix of narrow streets (sugar mill area) and wider new roads — compact Venue and i20 ideal
- **Agricultural routes** (towards Banswada, Yedapally): Some rough stretches — Venue or Creta ground clearance valuable
- **Seasonal heavy vehicles**: Sugar season brings heavy trucks on main roads — safer to be in an ADAS-equipped Hyundai
