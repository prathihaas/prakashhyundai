## What Is Maruti NEXA?

NEXA is Maruti Suzuki's premium dealership network, launched in 2015. NEXA sells:
- Baleno (hatchback)
- Fronx (micro-SUV)
- Grand Vitara (SUV)
- Ciaz (sedan — discontinued 2023)
- Jimny (off-roader)
- Invicto (rebadged Toyota Innova HyCross)

The NEXA experience differs from Arena (regular Maruti dealers) — premium showroom ambiance, better sales process. **But the cars are built in the same Maruti factories with the same Maruti quality standards.**
