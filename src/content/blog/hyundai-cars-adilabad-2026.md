## Adilabad Road Conditions — What to Drive

Adilabad district has a varied road landscape:
- **NH363** (Adilabad–Mancherial): Excellent 4-lane highway — any Hyundai model handles this effortlessly.
- **City roads**: Mixed quality through older parts of Adilabad town — good ground clearance helps. Venue (195mm) and Creta (190mm) are ideal.
- **Forest/Rural routes** (towards Utnoor, Sirpur): Some dirt roads remain. The Hyundai Venue and Creta handle these comfortably.
- **Godavari basin roads**: Occasional flooding repairs create rough patches. High ground clearance models are recommended.

**Our recommendation for Adilabad:**
- Budget under ₹10L: Hyundai Exter (185mm ground clearance, SUV styling)
- Budget ₹10–15L: Hyundai Venue (195mm, ADAS, compact size for city navigation)
- Budget ₹15L+: Hyundai Creta (190mm, 7 airbags, best-in-class safety)
