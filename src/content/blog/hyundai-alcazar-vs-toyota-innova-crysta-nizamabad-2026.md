## The Innova Crysta's Problems in 2026

### 1. Starting Price ₹20.77 Lakh vs Alcazar ₹16.78 Lakh

The Innova Crysta costs ₹4 lakh more at entry point. That's a significant gap. For ₹16.78L, you get the Alcazar with SUV styling, modern features, and ADAS. For ₹20.77L, you get an Innova Crysta with no ADAS and no sunroof.

### 2. Diesel Discontinued

Toyota discontinued the Innova Crysta diesel in India for BS6.2 compliance in 2023. The current Innova Crysta is petrol-only with a 2.7L engine delivering 10.26 km/l ARAI.

**Fuel cost at 15,000km/year:**
- Innova Crysta petrol (10.26 km/l): ₹1,46,200/year (₹100/litre)
- Alcazar diesel (17.5 km/l): ₹77,140/year (₹90/litre diesel)

**Alcazar saves approximately ₹69,000/year in fuel.** Over 5 years: ₹3.45 lakh in fuel savings.

### 3. No ADAS — Left Behind by Technology

Innova Crysta has no ADAS features at any price. No forward collision warning, no lane keep, no blind spot monitoring. For a family car at ₹20–27 lakh on a road like NH44, this is a significant safety gap vs the Alcazar's Level 2 ADAS.

### 4. Taxi Association

The Innova Crysta, while excellent, carries a strong taxi/commercial association in India. For private buyers wanting an SUV feel rather than an MPV/taxi feel, Alcazar delivers modern SUV presence. The Innova's monocoque-on-ladder-frame character is not for everyone.

**For commercial use** (rental fleet, cab services, large joint family use): Innova Crysta remains unmatched.
