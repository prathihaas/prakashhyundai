## The Ciaz Problem

Maruti discontinued the Ciaz in 2023 because it was losing ground to modern sedans like the Verna and Honda City. The core issues:

**1. No turbo power**: The Ciaz only had a naturally aspirated 1.5L engine making 105PS. The Verna offers a 160PS turbo petrol that dramatically changes the driving experience.

**2. Zero ADAS**: The Ciaz had no advanced safety features at any price point. No automatic emergency braking, no lane keep assist, no forward collision warning.

**3. Outdated platform**: The Ciaz's platform was developed in the 2010s. By 2022–23, it felt significantly behind the Verna's sportier, more modern architecture.

**4. Conservative design**: The Ciaz looked like an office car. The 2024+ Verna has bold, sporty styling that made it India's Car of the Year 2024.

**5. No connected car**: No OTA updates, no remote features, no SOS emergency calling.
