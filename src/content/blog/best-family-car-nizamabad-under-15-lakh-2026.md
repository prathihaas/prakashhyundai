## What Makes a Good Family Car for Nizamabad?

Before the list, here is what matters for Nizamabad families:

**Space:** A family of 4–5 needs comfortable seating for all — rear seat width, legroom, and headroom matter.

**Safety:** At minimum, 4 airbags, ABS, and rear parking sensors. ADAS features are a bonus.

**AC performance:** Telangana summers are extreme. Rear AC vents, powerful cooling, and ideally ventilated seats are important.

**Ground clearance:** Nizamabad's roads include city streets, semi-urban stretches, and occasional rural roads near Bodhan, Armoor, and Balkonda.

**Mileage:** Monthly fuel bills matter for a family budget. 14+ km/l real-world is the benchmark.

**After-sales support:** Long-term ownership is better with an authorized service centre in Nizamabad.
