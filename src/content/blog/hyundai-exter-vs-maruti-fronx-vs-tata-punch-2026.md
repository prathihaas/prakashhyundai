## Why Hyundai Exter Wins in Nizamabad

### 1. ADAS — Only Car in Segment with Advanced Safety

**The Hyundai Exter is the only micro-SUV offering ADAS features.** At ₹9.5–10L, the Exter SX(O) comes with:
- Forward Collision Avoidance (FCA)
- Lane Keeping Assist (LKA)
- High Beam Assist
- Driver Attention Warning

Neither the Maruti Fronx nor the Tata Punch offer any ADAS across any variant. For young Nizamabad buyers on highways, this is a genuine safety differentiator.

### 2. Maruti Fronx Has No Global NCAP Rating

This is a critical point Fronx buyers often miss. The Maruti Fronx has **not been tested by Global NCAP** as of 2026. It carries no safety star rating. The Hyundai Exter and Tata Punch have both received strong safety ratings.

For a family in Nizamabad buying a car for daily use and occasional highway runs, buying a car with no safety certification is a risk.

### 3. Best Boot Space in Segment

Hyundai Exter's 391-litre boot is the largest in the segment:
- Exter: 391 litres
- Tata Punch: 366 litres
- Maruti Fronx: 308 litres

For Nizamabad families who travel to Hyderabad for medical appointments or family functions, boot space matters.

### 4. Ground Clearance — Fronx Falls Short

The Maruti Fronx has only 150mm ground clearance — the lowest in this comparison. For Nizamabad's roads, district roads, and agricultural area routes:
- Exter: 185mm
- Punch: 187mm
- Fronx: 150mm

The Fronx's low ground clearance makes it significantly more vulnerable to Nizamabad's uneven urban roads and rural terrain. Speed breakers near schools and markets can be a problem.
